About

Personal information

I'm Sinenhlanhla Shezi, a 24 year-old female born and bred in Johannesburg. A creative at heart, I find joy in writing and exploring the intersection of technology and creativity.

Educational Background

I attended high school at Barnato Park Highschool, and in my final year, I pursued homeschooling , obtaining a GED in 2019- an equivalent qualification to the South African matric.

Work Experience

In 2019, alongside completing my GED, I began my professional journey as a part-time salon operator, engaging clients and assisting with salon treatments and appointments. Subsequently, in 2020, I transitioned to an administrative role at a college's head office in Midrand. Concurrently, I continued my part-time role as a salon operator. In this position, I facilitated admissions, supported students in navigating e-learning platforms, and participated in student recruitment efforts. It was during this time that my interest in technology was sparked, as our college sought solutions to transition to online learning amidst the challenges posed by the COVID-19 pandemic.

Projects and Future Endeavors

In 2023, I delved deeper into exploring various careers in the tech industry. This led me to discover organizations in South Africa offering bootcamps and courses focused on programming languages such as JavaScript, Python and #C. While I haven't yet completed any tech-related projects. I'm eagerly looking forward to the hands-on experiences and new skills I will gain through this bootcamp.
